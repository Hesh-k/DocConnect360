DocConnect360 is a revolutionary online platform developed by VirtualVitals, the team behind many acclaimed medical software products in Sri Lanka. It allows patients to connect with doctors through a largely straightforward process, which makes it possible for even the least tech savvy people to schedule appointments, obtain medications, and receive medical advice from the convenience of their own homes. DocConnect360 is popular for its vast network of highly skilled and professionally certified doctors specializing in a plethora of fields. So, no matter who you are, we have a match for you!

With DocConnect360, you can quickly search for doctors based on their specialties, locations, and schedules. And once you’ve obtained your prescription, you no longer have to suffer in queues or make unpleasant calls to your clinic or start an awkward WhatsApp chat with your pharmacy. Our latest addition, RxRunner, is here for you. Just upload your Rx (prescription)—or if it’s a recent appointment, leave a checkmark in “Run my Rx” before you pay your bill—and we’ll have your medications delivered right to your doorstep. Our network of RxRunners can deliver to every corner of the island, and it's completely free!

What makes DocConnect360 a game-changer is its user-friendly interface, secure payment method, and quick, 100% confidential service. But most of all, it’s the assurance patients have that their medical care is being provided by the most recognized experts in the field. Whether you're looking for a specialist or a general practitioner, DocConnect360 has you covered.
